About the Game

Cebu Quest is an educational game that teaches Cebu City's jeepney routes and traffic rules through fun, interactive gameplay. Navigate Colon Street, follow rules, and earn rewards while learning essential commuter skills!

Game Features Overview

Supply Merchant: The Supply Merchant provides players with consumables to restore health and mana and sells gear to enhance character stats.

Reward System Overview: Motivates players to complete tasks, progress through levels, and explore the game world. Rewards are granted upon completing tasks, quests, and answering short quizzes.

PVE System Overview: Offers engaging experiences like fighting mobs, exploring the map, and facing powerful bosses. Rewards include gold, Jeepney route information, and Rare-quality armor gear.

Quiz System: Adds an educational layer with topics related to directions around Colon, Cebu, and jeepney routes. Quizzes appear upon character death to reinforce knowledge.

Colon Cebu Map: Designed to mimic the layout of Colon Cebu, showcasing streets, intersections, landmarks, traffic signs, and road markings to offer a realistic representation.
